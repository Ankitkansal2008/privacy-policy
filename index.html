Privacy Policy - Sky Soar: Bird Rush

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sky Soar: Bird Rush. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile game.

2. Information We Collect

Sky Soar: Bird Rush is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your scores, unlocked characters, and coins are stored locally on your device using localStorage.

  • No Personal Information: We do NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve game functionality

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

5. Data Storage

All game data (scores, coins, unlocked birds) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Sky Soar: Bird Rush is suitable for all ages (3+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
